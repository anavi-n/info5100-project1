Women In The Workforce

The map below shows the percentage of adult females (15+ years) in the labor force in different countries. The darker the pink, the higher the percentage of women in that particular country.



The scatter plot below shows the female literacy rate on the x-axis and female labor percentage from the map above the y axis. It is interesting to see how countries countries considered to have high human development indices have high literacy rates and about average male-female participation in the labor force.
